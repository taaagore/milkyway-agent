# Lactometer Reading Thresholds (Proxy Method)

These values approximate the minimum lactometer reading for 
unadulterated milk, based on standard dairy science ranges 
(cow: 1.028-1.032 SG, buffalo: 1.030-1.034 SG).

Note: FSSAI's actual legal standard (FSS Food Products Standards 2011) specifies minimum SNF% (cow 8.5%, buffalo 9.0%) and fat%, not lactometer reading directly. Lactometer reading is used here as a simplified proxy for water dilution detection; full SNF compliance also requires fat-content testing,which is out of scope for this prototype.

MIN_LACTOMETER_READING_COW = 28.0
MIN_LACTOMETER_READING_BUFFALO = 30.0
